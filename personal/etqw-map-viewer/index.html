ET:QW Map Viewer

2007
View Project

Using a console command that would automatically take screenshots one tile at a time, I was able to pretty quickly get screenshots of all the maps in Quake Wars, minus one. It used some weird tricks to overlay multiple areas on the same coordinate space. The end result was a pretty cool look at what Megatexture could do at the time. Area 22 and Refinery are great examples, especially in the mountainous areas.
